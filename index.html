9/6 - Line Begins to Sell Smartphones That Can Be Used Inexpensively

Translated from here on 9/6/17

Line is a service that allows the sending of messages with a smartphone, etc. From the 5th of this month, Line has begun to sell smartphones that can be used inexpensively.

The cheapest usage charge for 1 month is 500￥. Regular phones cannot do that, but people who are using the line service can use their telephones and send messages. Even when using this service a lot, the service fee is similar.

In addition, [users] can transmit up to 1Gb. For emails, 1Gb can be sending and receiving about 2000 of them. That can be watching about 4 hours of Internet videos. A lot of companies are selling smartphones that can be used inexpensively, and competition looks like it is becoming more furious because of this.
